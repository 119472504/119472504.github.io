Travel Experience:

I have done a lot of travelling in my life, with family and friends, with school and different sporting clubs.
It is something that I am passionate about and look forward to no matter where I go.
I have travelled to loads of different countries and places such as,Canada, America, Italy, and have
done various activities such as skiing and mountain biking.

Canada:

I visited Canada during Summer 2018 and stayed there for 3 weeks. During that time I visted Vancouver and
Toronto, two of Canadas biggest cities. I went and saw the Canandian National Team play Sweden in an
internationl ice hockey friendly at the Scotiabank Arena in Toronto. After that I went to Vancouver and
during my time there I went mountain biking in Whistler, the mountain range 30 minutes away from downtown Vancouver.
During the winter months Whistler is completely covered in snow. The 2010 Winter Olympics were held there.

New York:

I went to New York in Christmas 2019 and it is still one of my favourite holidays of all time. I visited the
Empire State Building, the Statue of Liberty, I even went and saw a New York Knicks game with my brother
and father. Ice skating in Central Park was a fantastic experience and standing in the middle of Times
Square felt like standing on the heartbeat of the city itself.

Italy:

In my 4th year in secondary school, De La Salle organised a ski trip for the students. My friends and I
all jumped on the opportunity to go skiing. We went to Andalo, in Northern Italy, for a week. We had ski
lessons in the morning and afternoon and any time outside of the lessons we were allowed to go off skiing
by ourselves. It was one of the most enjoyable weeks I'd ever had during secondary school and would love
to be able to go on another trip with all my old friends.
